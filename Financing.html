in Eindhoven. From opening a Dutch bank account to learning about
student financing options, we've got you covered.

Opening A Dutch Bank Account

A Dutch Bank Account Is Crucial For Paying Rent, Groceries, And More.

Steps To Open A Bank Account:

  • 1. Make An Appointment With A Bank (E.G., ING, ABN AMRO, Rabobank).
  • 2. Bring The Following Documents:
    • Passport Or ID.
    • Proof Of Address (Housing Contract).
    • BSN Number (From Municipality Registration).
  • 3. Complete The Application And Receive Your Debit Card.

Student Financing (Mijn DUO)

Mijn DUO is a government financing program for students. EU students may qualify for financial aid or loans.

Steps To Apply:

  1. Register with Mijn DUO DUO Website.
  2. Provide necessary documents:
    • Proof of enrollment at a Dutch university.
    • Proof of residence in the Netherlands.
  3. Choose between financial aid or a student loan.

Tips:

  • Payments are usually monthly.
  • Interest rates are low and student-friendly.
