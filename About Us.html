About Us

Welcome to our website! We are a group of passionate individuals
dedicated to helping international students settle in Eindhoven with
ease. Our goal is to provide you with all the information you need
to make your stay smooth and enjoyable.

To Simplify The Transition For Students Moving To Eindhoven.

To Create A Supportive Community For International Students.
