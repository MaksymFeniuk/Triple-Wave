Biking

Biking is the most popular way to get around Eindhoven.

Options:

  • Rent a bike: Check bike rental shops like Swapfiets.
  • Buy a used bike: Visit local bike markets or online platforms.

Tips:

  • Always lock your bike securely.
  • Invest in a good-quality second-hand bike for long-term use.
