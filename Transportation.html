Transportation in Eindhoven

Eindhoven offers various transportation options, including buses, trains, and biking. Whether you're a local or a newcomer, getting around the city is easy and convenient.

Know More

OV-chipkaart

The OV-chipkaart is a smart card used for public transport in the Netherlands. It works on buses, trains, and trams.

How to get your OV-chipkaart:

  1. Visit OV-chipkaart official website.
  2. Choose between an anonymous or personal card.
  3. Load credit and start traveling!

Benefits:

  • Easy access to all public transport.
  • Discounted rates for students and frequent travelers.

Biking

Biking is the most popular way to get around Eindhoven.

Options:

  • Rent a bike: Check bike rental shops like Swapfiets.
  • Buy a used bike: Visit local bike markets or online platforms.

Tips:

  • Always lock your bike securely.
  • Invest in a good-quality second-hand bike for long-term use.
